About

Ancient Olympic celebrates its first birthday. You can jump in a huge kitchen, try to be in finish as fast as possible. Increasing degree of difficulty. This map includes easy jumps but also difficult parkour elements. You can play with your friends and also alone. The first stage is a kitchen where you have to jump from cupboard to table and other kitchen machines. Reach the birthday cake at the top of the table to get teleportet into the candy land. It is the second stage. Where you have to jump across platforms at the top of a beatiful, tall and huge birthday cake. Stand on the candle to finish the parkour race. Celebrate Ancient Olympic's first birthday with this exiting parkour map, and enjoy your time. Learn different jump skills in this adventure.
